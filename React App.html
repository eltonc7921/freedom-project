Physics

To study physics, is to use math in order to make a predictive model of our world that we live in.

Gravity

Gravity as described by Newton
is proportional to the mass of objects
and inversely proportional to the distance
between those objects

The formula is as follows
F = G*m1*m2/(r^2)
where G is the gravitational constant

Motion

Newton's first law of motion
A body at rest will remain at rest and a body in motion will remain in motion unless acted upon an external force

Newton's second law of motion
F = ma, Force = mass * acceleration

Newton's third law of motion
When a body exerts a force on another body, that other body excerts an opposite force of the same magnitude

Although simple, these three laws are the building blocks of a large part of physics.
